Farmer's Market Access App

Smart India Hackathon 2024 - Problem Statement ID: 1637

Project Overview

We developed mobile application for agripreneurs that enables farmers to showcase their products, connect with stakeholders and buyers, and manage their product listings directly, thereby eliminating the need for intermediaries. The platform connects key stakeholders, including farmers, buyers, KVKs (Krishi Vigyan Kendra), FPOs (Farmer Producer Organizations), and APMCs (Agricultural Produce Market Committees).

Objectives

  • Develop a user-centric application to address challenges such as weather-related damages, time inefficiencies, and high transportation costs
  • Facilitate access to affordable and diverse seed varieties, including rare and heritage types
  • Empower farmers by enabling direct contact with buyers, bypassing intermediaries
  • Provide real-time market price information and quick adjustment to market demands

Unique Features

  • Daily Market Price Updates
  • Price Negotiation System
  • Seed Trade Marketplace
  • Auction System
  • Transportation Facilitation
  • Pre-Booking Options
  • Secure Payment Integration

Core Modules

Direct Purchase

Allows buyers to browse and sort items by location, product type, and price range, providing farmers with broader market access and increasing sales opportunities.

Subscription Services

Offers fresh vegetables and milk delivery services, ensuring steady income for farmers and convenient access for consumers while reducing middlemen involvement.

Negotiation Module

Enables direct price negotiation through chat and calls, ensuring fair deals between buyers and farmers through transparent communication.

Auction System

Facilitates both physical and e-auctions with video conferencing capabilities, allowing real-time bidding for optimal price discovery.

Technical Implementation

  • Flutter framework for cross-platform mobile development
  • Firebase for backend services and real-time updates
  • Stripe for secure payment processing
  • Integration with third-party logistics services

Feasibility Analysis

The project demonstrates strong technical feasibility due to widespread smartphone access and improving rural internet connectivity. The platform addresses trust and security concerns through secure payment gateways and transparent pricing mechanisms.

Future Scope

  • Real-time Quality and Grading System integration
  • AI-driven price prediction models
  • Predictive analytics for market demand
  • AI-powered product recommendations
